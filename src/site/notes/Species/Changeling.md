A **Changeling** is an [[Species/Altered Human\|altered human]] that can change their physical form at will to any humanoid shape. Their form can be any species of altered human, and though they do not gain the species [[Magic/Geas\|Geas]] of that species, their form is actually physically real and functioning. This means a changeling never needs glasses, never needs to lose weight, and never gets scars.  
There are limits, however. While bone structure can be changed to a degree, it cannot mend broken bones, and while scars can be removed, wounds cannot.  
While muscle size can be increased, a changeling still needs to work out to get stronger. 

In their true form, changelings are indistinguishable from each other. Their skin is perfectly smooth and white like marble, as is their hair. In their true form, they are biologically neither male nor female.  
Changelings cannot digest meat, and ruminate. Changelings don’t get old, but they die when they reach an age of exactly 55.
  
Changelings were probably created by ancient arcanists to spy on others.  
Because of this, changelings are rarely looked upon favorably.  
They live either amongst humans or in small communities at the edges of cities. There are also a few documented changeling villages, though.  
Because a changeling can change biological sex at will, changeling culture doesn’t have the concept of gender.
