A **Halfling** is a species of [[Species/Altered Human\|altered human]] that is known for their cuisine and affinity with agriculture.

Halflings are named after their size, half as tall as a human. 
According to their folklore, their kind was originally created to work in massive workshops, and get into small spaces where humans couldn’t.
This is reflected by their small size, double elbows, and eagerness to work. 
Apparently though, their creator had failed to embed subservience, and after the ancient civilization had fallen, and the altered humans roamed free, halflings immediately learned all there is to know about agriculture with the technology that was available, and they have not progressed much since, due to their inherent lack of ambition. It is because of this lack of ambition that they, even though they possess a [[Pathway\|Pathway]] from birth, almost never pursue magic.

Instead, they live simple lives on their farms, growing their crops and perfecting their stew. Their cuisine is famous throughout the empire, and it is rare to find a tavern, inn or restaurant without a halfling among the people working there.
