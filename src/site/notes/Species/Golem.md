A **Golem** is a [[Species/Homunculus\|homunculus]] made of soil, mud, or sometimes rocks. Their core is usually a piece of paper or something of the like, with instructions on it.  
Golems possess just enough sentience to fulfill simple tasks, like guarding a door or carrying goods. Golems can regenerate as much as needed as long as they have access to their building material and as long as their core is intact.  
Theoretically, any material can be used to make a golem, but because they need the same material to regenerate, usually soil is used because of its abundance. 
  
Golems can’t see very well, and are, as mentioned, incredibly stupid. The instructions need to be relatively simple, concise, and unambiguous. They cannot exist for very long, as the magic holding them together usually fades after a few days.
