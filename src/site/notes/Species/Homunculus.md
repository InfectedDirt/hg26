A **Homunculus** is a creature created through the use of magic, be it an animated book, a [[Species/Golem\|golem]], or a [[Species/Dwarf\|dwarf]]. Some have built-in methods of reproduction, but most don’t, and are created for a specific use.  
Unless provided, they do not have a spirit nor soul, though they do always have a mind.  
They never have a [[Pathway\|Pathway]] and as far as current arcane research shows, they cannot make one through conventional means.
