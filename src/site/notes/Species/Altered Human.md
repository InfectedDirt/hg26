An **Altered Human** is a human with a [[Magic/Geas#Pathway Geasa\|Pathway Geas]] that changes their physiology. Because Pathways are inherited, so is the species Geas, and because you can only inherit the Pathway in its entirety, there are no “half-bloods”. The child of a human and an altered human is either their father’s species or their mother’s species, never a mix.
