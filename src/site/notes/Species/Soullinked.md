A **Soullinked** human is a human that is telepathically linked to another human. 
Soullinked humans are different from other [[Species/Altered Human\|altered humans]] in the way the trait is inherited. Arcanists aren’t sure how exactly the species [[Magic/Geas\|Geas]] works, but it seems to not be bound to the [[Magic/Pathway\|Pathway]], but be bound to another thing entirely that is not yet understood.
It is inherited independently from Pathway and genes, and it doesn’t always manifest, making it hard to track. It can be inherited in addition to another species Geas.
When it manifests, a link is created between two soullinked people, usually of around the same age. They can see what the other sees, hear what the other hears, feel what the other feels, and communicate telepathically. 
It is possible that more links appear later, but often it stays at two per network.
