A **Gnome** is, allegedly, a [[Species/Homunculus\|homunculus]], and is known for being a household nuisance.
Gnomes live for whimsy. They are approximately half a metre tall, with a lot of that height consisting of their pointy hat, which is presumably part of them in some way.  
They live for as long as they are silly and interesting. Once they get bored, they grow old and die.  
Gnomes also seem to have some magical abilities, being able to slightly alter reality for the bit.

It is unknown who or how gnomes were created, but we know they weren’t created by the ancient arcanists, as all of their creations serve a clear purpose, which gnomes clearly don’t.

They can reproduce, but that might be an accidental feature, because their method of reproduction is simple: after all, what’s more whimsy than one gnome? That’s right, two gnomes!
