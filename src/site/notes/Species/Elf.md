An **Elf** is an [[Species/Altered Human\|altered human]] with an incredibly long lifespan.
The life of an elf is bound to that of a tree, their Lifeblood Tree. They live as long as the tree lives, and the tree lives as long as they live. This means their lifespans can reach hundreds of years, depending on their tree. It also means that if the tree dies, the elf dies, no matter how healthy they were.  
Elves have a deep connection to trees. They can often even understand and talk to trees, though trees don’t often have a lot to say.  

Elves do not need to sleep, but to maintain their connection to their [[Lifeblood Tree\|Lifeblood Tree]], elves need to bond with their tree every now and then. Sedentary elves usually just do this daily or weekly, but elves can easily survive for a few months without bonding. To bond, the elf essentially becomes a physical part of their tree. It usually takes around 8 hours, but after long periods without bonding, especially if the elf was far away, it can take weeks, in rare cases even years.

Elves cannot produce much fat tissue, so while they do not need to worry about becoming overweight, unlike humans, they cannot survive very long without food, with long-term starvation symptoms usually starting after approximately a week(assuming they do have water available). 

In the Empire, elves live in small communities near or in forests, though they may also be found in cities if there are trees.
