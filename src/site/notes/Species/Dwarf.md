A **Dwarf** is a [[Species/Homunculus\|Homunculus]] created out of stone and brought to life. What is unique about them is that unlike most homunculi, they do have a soul, though they still do not have a spirit, or at least not a spirit in a way comprehensible to humans.

The other thing that makes them unique, is that they are able to reproduce, and that the new dwarves possess a fully functional and individual new soul and mind. To reproduce, a dwarf carves a new dwarf out of a rock. The eyes are carved last, and when they are finished, the parent dwarf must chant the Dwarven Birth Hymn, which dwarves know from birth, for a full night, from dusk till dawn, to bring the dwarf to life.

Dwarves live in the Borderlands, which they call the Alps, presumably the ancient name for the mountain range. Most live underground.
They were originally created by an ancient arcanist who sought to improve mankind in some way. That is all that the dwarves can still tell us.
